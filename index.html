THE PROBLEM

SEND MONEY HOME FAST

Semofa grew out of the frustration faced by family members living abroad not being able to easily support family and friends back home.

The idea came from the founder of Semofa who struggled to support family and friends back home while working full-time as most transfer merchants were far away and required time taken out of his day in order to complete.

The founder wanted to regularly send a monthly allowance to his grandmother from an app on his phone without having to find and visit a merchant in the city.

THE SOLUTION

SELECT | INVITE | SEND

Semofa was developed to allow family members living abroad to quickly and easily send gifts and treats back home to friends and family.

After creating your free account, search for and select the gift you would like to send from our list of preferred partners, invite other friends or family members to contribute to the gift or send solo, add a message and instantly send.

Best of all, both sender and recipient can earn loyalty points from our business partners. These points entitle you to discounts and other rewards.
